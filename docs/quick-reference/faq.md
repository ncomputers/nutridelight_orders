# Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

## General Questions

### What is Nutridelight Orders?
Nutridelight Orders is a web-based platform for restaurant produce ordering and management. It enables restaurants to place daily orders for fresh produce while providing suppliers with comprehensive order management, purchase planning, and invoicing capabilities.

### Who can use the system?
- **Restaurants**: Place daily produce orders
- **Admin Staff**: Manage orders, restaurants, and system operations
- **Purchase Team**: Plan and execute stock purchases
- **Sales Team**: Handle invoicing and payment collection

### What are the system requirements?
- Modern web browser (Chrome, Firefox, Safari, Edge)
- Internet connection
- No software installation required
- Mobile-friendly interface

## Ordering Questions

### How do restaurants place orders?
1. Navigate to your restaurant's order URL
2. Browse available items by category
3. Enter desired quantities
4. Add special notes if needed
5. Submit order before 11:00 PM deadline

### What is the order deadline?
Orders must be placed by **11:00 PM** for next-day delivery. Orders placed after the deadline will be processed for the following day.

### How are prices determined?
Prices are based on daily market rates (mandi prices) and may vary based on:
- Current market availability
- Seasonal factors
- Quality grades
- Restaurant tier and volume

### Can I modify or cancel an order?
- **Pending orders**: Can be modified or cancelled until 10:00 PM
- **Confirmed orders**: Cancellation fee of 10% applies
- **Delivered orders**: Cannot be cancelled

### What happens if items are unavailable?
If items become unavailable after order placement:
- You'll be notified by 7:00 AM
- Alternative items may be suggested
- Order may be partially fulfilled
- No charge for unavailable items

## Payment and Invoicing

### When do I receive an invoice?

## Troubleshooting

### Order page not loading
1. Check internet connection
2. Verify URL is correct
3. Clear browser cache
4. Try different browser
5. Contact support if issue persists

### Items showing as unavailable
1. Check order deadline (11:00 PM cutoff)
2. Verify item availability status
3. Contact support for special requests
4. Try again later for stock updates

### Can't submit order
1. Verify all required fields are filled
2. Check quantities are valid numbers
3. Ensure internet connection is stable
4. Try refreshing the page
5. Contact support if needed

### Invoice not received
1. Check spam/junk folder
2. Verify email address is correct
3. Contact sales team
4. Request invoice resend

### Payment not reflecting
1. Wait 24 hours for processing
2. Check payment reference number
3. Contact sales team with payment details
4. Provide payment proof if needed
