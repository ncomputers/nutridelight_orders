# Sales and Invoicing Guide

## Overview

This guide covers the sales invoicing and payment management functions within the Nutridelight Orders system, designed for the sales team to handle billing, collections, and financial reporting.

## Access and Authentication

### Sales Team Access
1. Navigate to `/sales`
2. Enter sales team credentials
3. Access sales dashboard
4. View assigned accounts

### Sales Panel Features
- Invoice generation and management
- Payment tracking and collections
- Customer account management
- Financial reporting and analytics

## Invoice Management

### Invoice Generation Process

#### 1. Order Selection
- Navigate to "Create Invoice" section
- Select delivered orders for invoicing
- Filter by date range, restaurant, or status
- Review order details and items

#### 2. Invoice Creation
1. Choose orders to include in invoice
2. Verify quantities and unit prices
3. Apply discounts or special pricing
4. Add additional charges if applicable
5. Generate invoice with unique number

#### 3. Invoice Details
- **Invoice Number**: Auto-generated unique identifier
- **Restaurant**: Customer information and billing address
- **Order Items**: Detailed line items with quantities and prices
- **Pricing**: Unit prices, totals, and calculations
- **Taxes**: Applicable taxes and fees
- **Terms**: Payment terms and due dates

### Invoice Components

#### Header Information
- Invoice number and date
- Restaurant billing details
- Purchase order references
- Payment terms

#### Line Items
- Item descriptions and codes
- Quantities delivered
- Unit prices and extensions
- Item categories and taxes

#### Financial Summary
- Subtotal calculations
- Discount applications
- Tax computations
- Grand total and due amount

#### Payment Terms
- Due date calculation
- Payment methods accepted
- Late payment penalties
- Early payment discounts

### Invoice Status Tracking

#### Status Flow
1. **Draft**: Invoice being prepared
2. **Sent**: Invoice delivered to customer
3. **Partial**: Partial payment received
4. **Paid**: Full payment collected
5. **Overdue**: Payment past due date
6. **Disputed**: Customer dispute in progress

#### Status Management
- Manual status updates
