# Purchase Management Guide

## Overview

This guide covers the purchase planning and stock management functions within the Nutridelight Orders system, designed for the purchase team to efficiently manage procurement operations.

## Access and Authentication

### Purchase Team Login
1. Navigate to `/purchase/login`
2. Enter purchase team credentials
3. Select user role and responsibilities
4. Session management with auto-timeout

### Purchase Panel Access
- URL: `/purchase`
- Requires purchase team authentication
- Dedicated purchase management interface
- Real-time stock and demand data

## Dashboard Overview

### Main Sections
- **Demand Aggregation**: View total customer requirements
- **Stock Management**: Monitor current inventory levels
- **Purchase Planning**: Create and manage purchase orders
- **Supplier Management**: Coordinate with suppliers
- **Stock History**: Track inventory movements

### Key Metrics
- Total daily demand by item

- Cost variance tracking

## Demand Aggregation

### Viewing Customer Demand
1. Access "Demand Aggregation" section
2. Filter by date range and status
3. View item-wise requirements
4. Analyze category-wise demand

### Demand Analysis Features
- **Total Requirements**: Sum of all confirmed orders
- **Category Breakdown**: Vegetables, Herbs, Fruits
- **Time Analysis**: Daily, weekly, monthly trends
- **Restaurant Breakdown**: Demand by customer
